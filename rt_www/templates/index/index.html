Welcome To Red Tide

Master's Swimming NYC

Red Tide masters swim team is a community of athletes with a passion for the water. We swim for fun, fitness and competition.

A New York City masters team for over twenty years, Red Tide prides itself on the many walks of life that comprise our membership -- from novice swimmers to USMS national and world titleholders as well as runners, cyclists and triathletes. Our members are locally based to globetrotting professionals who enjoy the camaraderie and competition that are part of Red Tide.


Visiting the City?

Join us for a workout

We welcome out-of-town swimmers to join us during any of our practices. The fee is $30 per session payable to the coach on deck. Check out our Schedule for more information on practice times and pool locations.
